About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher specializing in neutron star mergers (NSMs). My primary area of research is the analysis of gravitational waves (GWs) emitted in the inspiral and post-merger phase of NSMs. To achieve this, I carry out large-scale hydrodynamical simulations of NSMs in numerical relativity. I am also actively involved in the development of methods for the construction of faithful templates for the GW emission of NSM remnants.
